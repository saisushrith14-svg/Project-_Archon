Risk Management

Protect your capital and trade smarter with risk control, position sizing, and disciplined planning.

Introduction

Risk Management teaches you how to protect your capital, limit losses, and build consistency in trading.

This course focuses on the practical tools and mindset needed to trade with discipline rather than relying on luck or emotion.

You will learn how to size positions, manage stops, and preserve gains while maintaining a sustainable trading plan.

What You'll Learn

  • Why risk management is the foundation of every successful trading strategy.
  • How to calculate position size for each trade.
  • How to use stop loss and take profit orders effectively.
  • Why risk-to-reward ratio matters for long-term success.
  • Portfolio diversification and capital preservation techniques.
  • Managing emotions to avoid impulsive, high-risk behavior.
  • How to set daily loss limits and avoid overtrading.
  • The value of trade journaling for continuous improvement.
  • Building a repeatable trading plan that supports consistency.
  • Common mistakes traders make and how to avoid them.
  • How to balance leverage with proper risk controls.
  • Maintaining discipline through drawdowns and winning streaks.

Who This Course Is For

Beginners and intermediate traders who want to stop gambling and start protecting their account value.

Prerequisites

Basic trading knowledge or completion of a beginner course. This course is ideal for anyone who wants to trade more responsibly.

Course Outcomes

  • Manage capital so one bad trade does not ruin your account.
  • Determine position sizes based on risk limits.
  • Place stop loss and take profit levels with confidence.
  • Build a risk-aware trading plan.
  • Use daily loss limits to protect performance.
  • Keep a disciplined trading journal and review trades.
  • Reduce emotional bias and overtrading.
  • Develop the skills to trade consistently over time.
