Algorithmic Trading with Python

An advanced engineering-led course for traders who want to automate strategy execution and test ideas at scale.

Course Overview

In this advanced track, you will convert strategy hypotheses into Python workflows, backtest against historical data, and evaluate risk-adjusted performance before live deployment. The focus is on reliability, not just signal generation.

Skills You'll Learn

  • Structuring trading bots with reusable Python modules
  • Backtesting and validating assumptions with clean datasets
  • Building safeguards for drawdown, slippage, and volatility spikes
  • Monitoring live strategy health and execution logs
