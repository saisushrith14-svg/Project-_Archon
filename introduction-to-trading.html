Introduction to Trading

A beginner-friendly cohort that explains how global markets function and how to place your first structured trades.

Course Overview

This course is designed for new traders who want clarity before taking risk. You will learn how different asset classes behave, how to interpret order flow, and how to build a repeatable trading routine with risk controls from day one.

Skills You'll Learn

  • Reading market structure and identifying trend context
  • Placing market, limit, and stop orders with confidence
  • Building a simple pre-trade and post-trade checklist
  • Applying position sizing and basic risk limits
