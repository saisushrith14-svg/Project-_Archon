Crypto Basics

Learn the fundamentals of cryptocurrency, blockchain, wallets, and how to get started safely.

Introduction

Crypto Basics is designed for new traders who want to understand the building blocks of digital currency, the blockchain networks that power them, and the safest ways to participate in the crypto economy.

From Bitcoin and Ethereum to wallets, gas fees, and smart contracts, this course walks you through the core concepts step-by-step so you can move beyond hype and trade with confidence.

By the end, you will know how to send, receive, store, and protect crypto using real-world best practices.

What You'll Learn

  • What blockchain is and why decentralization matters.
  • How Bitcoin differs from Ethereum and other altcoins.
  • The role of stablecoins and when to use them.
  • Hot wallets versus cold wallets and security tradeoffs.
  • How private keys and seed phrases keep your funds safe.
  • Understanding gas fees and transaction costs.
  • How to buy, send, and receive crypto securely.
  • Common crypto scams and how to avoid them.
  • Security best practices for everyday crypto use.
  • An introduction to DeFi, NFTs, and Web3 applications.
  • Using blockchain explorers to verify transactions.
  • How to build a simple, safe crypto setup for beginners.

Who This Course Is For

New investors, curious professionals, and anyone who wants to understand crypto without relying on buzzwords or speculation.

Prerequisites

Basic internet familiarity and a desire to learn about digital money. No prior trading or blockchain experience required.

Course Outcomes

  • Confidently explain how blockchain works.
  • Differentiate between Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoins.
  • Use wallets safely and manage private keys.
  • Estimate and reduce gas fees correctly.
  • Detect common scams and protect your funds.
  • Navigate DeFi, NFTs, and Web3 fundamentals.
  • Verify transactions with blockchain explorers.
  • Operate within a secure beginner crypto workflow.
