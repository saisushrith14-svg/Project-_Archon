Our Story

Built by Traders, For Traders

Founded in 2024, Archon was born out of frustration with existing trading platforms. They were either too complex for beginners or lacked the professional tools required by institutional traders. We believed there had to be a better way.

We set out to build a platform that bridges the gap. By combining institutional-grade infrastructure with a beautiful, intuitive interface, we created a space where anyone can trade Crypto, Forex, and Stocks seamlessly.

Today, Archon serves over 2 million users across 150 countries, processing billions in daily trading volume with zero downtime.
