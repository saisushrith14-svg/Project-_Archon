Technical Analysis

Build the skills to read charts, identify trends, and make informed trade decisions with technical indicators.

Introduction

Technical Analysis teaches traders how to interpret price action, charts, and momentum signals so they can identify high-probability trading opportunities.

This course blends classic charting techniques with modern indicator analysis, helping you build a disciplined approach to entries, exits, and risk management.

Whether you're trading crypto, stocks, or forex, you will learn how to read the market with clarity and purpose.

What You'll Learn

  • What Technical Analysis is and how it works.
  • How to read candlestick charts and interpret patterns.
  • Support and resistance zones for smarter trade planning.
  • Creating and validating trendlines.
  • Identifying chart patterns like triangles, flags, and double tops.
  • Using moving averages for trend direction and cross signals.
  • Applying RSI and MACD to price momentum and divergence.
  • Analyzing volume to confirm moves or spot exhaustion.
  • Fibonacci retracement levels for potential reversal areas.
  • Entry and exit strategies with realistic stop placement.
  • Multi-timeframe analysis to align higher timeframe structure.
  • Avoiding false breakouts and spotting trap setups.

Who This Course Is For

Traders with some market exposure who want to move past guesswork and read charts like a professional.

Prerequisites

Basic familiarity with trading terminology and a willingness to practice chart analysis. No advanced math required.

Course Outcomes

  • Analyze charts and identify trending and ranging conditions.
  • Spot support and resistance levels before price reaches them.
  • Use moving averages, RSI, MACD, and volume as confirmation tools.
  • Apply Fibonacci retracements to realistic trade ideas.
  • Construct entry and exit plans with proper risk/reward ratios.
  • Filter false breakouts and avoid low-probability trades.
  • Make decisions using multi-timeframe alignment.
  • Recognize practical chart setups for real markets.
